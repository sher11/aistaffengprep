Value-Story Mapping

How to Map Stories ▼
  1. Identify values: List 3-5 core company values
  2. Review your stories: Which demonstrate each value?
  3. Adapt framing: Same story, different emphasis
  4. Use their language: Mirror company terminology

Authenticity Matters

Key Principle

Don't fake alignment. If your values don't match, it's better for both sides. Focus on:

  • Finding genuine connections
  • Being honest about what excites you
  • Asking about areas you want to learn more

Questions to Ask

Company-Specific Questions ▼

For Stripe:

  • "How does the team balance API stability with iteration speed?"
  • "What's a recent decision where 'users first' created an interesting trade-off?"

For Netflix:

  • "Can you share an example of 'freedom and responsibility' in practice?"
  • "How does the team handle disagreements on technical direction?"

For OpenAI/Anthropic:

  • "How does safety consideration affect day-to-day engineering decisions?"
  • "What's an example of a project where you had to balance speed with safety?"

Pre-Interview Checklist

Identified 3-5 company values
Mapped stories to each value
Prepared company-specific story framings
Prepared 3-5 thoughtful questions
Researched recent company news
