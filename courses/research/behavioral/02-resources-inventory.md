# Behavioral Rounds - Resources Inventory

## Primary Resources

### 1. StaffEngPrep.com - Behavioral Section
- **Link**: https://staffengprep.com/prepguide/
- **Summary**: Foundation guide covering STAR method, staff-level competencies, and common behavioral questions for senior engineering roles.
- **Key Takeaways**: Focus on influence without authority, technical leadership stories, handling ambiguity
- **Difficulty**: Beginner to Intermediate
- **Estimated Time**: 2-3 hours

### 2. The Manager's Path (Book)
- **Author**: Camille Fournier
- **Summary**: Essential reading on engineering career progression from IC to management. Chapters on staff+ roles provide insight into expected competencies.
- **Key Takeaways**: Staff engineers operate organizationally, need strong communication, must drive alignment
- **Difficulty**: Intermediate
- **Estimated Time**: 8-10 hours

### 3. Staff Engineer (Book)
- **Author**: Will Larson
- **Link**: https://staffeng.com/book
- **Summary**: Comprehensive guide to staff+ engineering roles, archetypes, and operating models.
- **Key Takeaways**: Four staff archetypes (Tech Lead, Architect, Solver, Right Hand), sponsorship vs mentorship
- **Difficulty**: Intermediate
- **Estimated Time**: 6-8 hours

### 4. An Elegant Puzzle (Book)
- **Author**: Will Larson
- **Summary**: Systems thinking for engineering management. Applicable to staff engineers who need to understand organizational dynamics.
- **Key Takeaways**: Organizations as systems, sizing teams, managing technical debt
- **Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced
- **Estimated Time**: 8-10 hours

## Academic/Research Resources

### Psychological Frameworks

#### Emotional Intelligence (EQ)
- **Resource**: Daniel Goleman's work on EQ
- **Summary**: Framework for understanding self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills
- **Application**: Underpins effective behavioral responses about conflict, collaboration, and leadership

#### Growth Mindset
- **Resource**: Carol Dweck's research
- **Summary**: Fixed vs growth mindset framework
- **Application**: Answering failure and growth questions effectively

### Communication Frameworks

#### Crucial Conversations (Book)
- **Authors**: Patterson, Grenny, McMillan, Switzler
- **Summary**: Framework for high-stakes conversations
- **Application**: Stories about conflict resolution, difficult feedback, stakeholder management
- **Time**: 6-8 hours

#### Nonviolent Communication (Book)
- **Author**: Marshall Rosenberg
- **Summary**: Observation, feeling, need, request framework
- **Application**: Describing how you communicated in difficult situations
- **Time**: 6-8 hours

## Tools & Templates

### Story Preparation

#### Notion Templates
- Various free templates for STAR story organization
- Search "behavioral interview template Notion"

#### Spreadsheet Tracker
- Create a matrix: Stories x Categories
- Track which stories cover which competencies
- Note company alignment for each story

### Practice Tools

#### Recording Practice
- Use phone/computer to record yourself
- Review for filler words, timing, clarity
- Target 2-3 minutes per story

#### Timer Apps
- Practice with countdown timer
- Build internal sense of pacing
