## Practice Problems

### Story Writing Exercises

**Exercise 1:** Write a STAR story about a technical decision you made. Include:
- Context in under 30 words
- Your specific task
- 3-5 specific actions YOU took
- Quantified result and one key learning

**Exercise 2:** Write a conflict resolution story following the framework:
- Who was the conflict with and why
- Your internal process
- Resolution approach with specific conversation points
- Outcome and relationship afterward

**Exercise 3:** Write a failure story that:
- Owns the failure without excuses
- Describes impact honestly
- Shows concrete learning
- Demonstrates changed behavior

### Mock Interview Questions

**Set A (Beginner):**
1. Tell me about a recent technical decision you made.
2. Describe a time you helped a team member.
3. What's a project you're proud of?

**Set B (Intermediate):**
1. Tell me about a disagreement with a colleague.
2. Describe your biggest professional mistake.
3. How have you improved code quality on your team?

**Set C (Advanced):**
1. Tell me about influencing a decision without authority.
2. Describe leading a cross-team initiative.
3. How do you handle it when leadership disagrees with your technical recommendation?

## Self-Evaluation Rubrics

### STAR Response Rubric

| Criteria | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Adequate) | 5 (Strong) |
|----------|----------|--------------|------------|
| Structure | Disorganized | Basic STAR | Clear STAR with good pacing |
| Specificity | Vague | Some details | Specific examples and numbers |
| Action Focus | "We" language | Mix of I/we | Clear "I" with specific actions |
| Results | None or vague | Mentioned | Quantified with learnings |
| Time | Too long/short | Roughly 2-3 min | Well-paced 2-3 minutes |

### Story Quality Rubric

| Criteria | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Adequate) | 5 (Strong) |
|----------|----------|--------------|------------|
| Relevance | Wrong category | Partially fits | Perfect fit for question |
| Impact | Trivial | Moderate | Significant, staff-level |
| Learning | None shown | Mentioned | Deep reflection shown |
| Authenticity | Seems rehearsed | Somewhat natural | Genuine and engaging |
