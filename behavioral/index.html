Time Allocation

For a 2-3 minute response:

  • Situation (15-20 sec): Brief context, stakes
  • Task (15-20 sec): Your role, challenges
  • Action (90-120 sec): Specific steps YOU took
  • Result (30-40 sec): Outcomes, metrics, learnings
